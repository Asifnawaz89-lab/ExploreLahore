Lahori Chargha – The Ultimate Spicy Chicken Delight

Lahori CHargha

Overview

Lahori Chargha is one of Lahore’s most famous traditional dishes — a whole chicken marinated in a rich blend of spices, deep-fried to golden perfection, and bursting with bold flavors. This crispy and juicy delicacy is a must-try for anyone wanting to experience authentic Lahori cuisine.

What is Lahori Chargha?

Chargha is a whole chicken, cleaned and marinated with a mix of yogurt, garlic, ginger, red chili, garam masala, lemon juice, and other secret spices. It’s then steamed or partially cooked before being deep-fried, resulting in crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat inside.

Why is Lahori Chargha Special?

  • Unique Flavor: The secret marinade creates a spicy, tangy, and aromatic profile that sets it apart from other fried chicken recipes.
  • Crispy Texture: The double cooking method ensures a crispy exterior while keeping the meat juicy and tender.
  • Street Food Favorite: Available at popular food streets like Gawalmandi and Fort Road Food Street in Lahore.
  • Perfect Accompaniment: Usually served with naan, mint chutney, and sliced onions.

Where to Try Lahori Chargha?

  • Famous Chargha House, Gawalmandi
  • Butt Karahi & Chargha, Lakshmi Chowk
  • Chargha Hub, Fortress Area
  • Local street vendors in the Walled City of Lahore

Best Time to Eat

Lahori Chargha is perfect for lunch, dinner, or a late-night snack. It’s especially popular during festivals and family gatherings.

Final Thoughts

If you want to taste the real flavors of Lahore, Lahori Chargha is a dish you can’t miss. Its bold spices, crispy skin, and juicy meat make it a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
