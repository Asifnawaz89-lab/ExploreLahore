When it comes to authentic Pakistani cuisine, especially Karahi, no name is as iconic in Lahore as Butt Karahi. Known for its rich flavor, tender meat, and sizzling presentation, Butt Karahi has become a must-visit food destination for both locals and tourists.

What Makes It Special?

  • Live Cooking: The karahi is cooked fresh right in front of you in traditional iron woks over an open flame.
  • Simple Ingredients, Big Flavor: With just meat, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, butter, and a few spices, the flavor is bold and unforgettable.
  • Generous Portions: Perfect for sharing with friends or family.
